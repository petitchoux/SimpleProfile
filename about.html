From former cook to software developer.

In 2016, I graduated from the International Culinary Center. Soon after, I found a bigger passion that I wanted to pursue: tech. Tech has enabled me to express my creativity and ideas through software development. In the spring of 2019, I completed CodePath's iOS development course and am currently a member of CUNY Tech Prep, a web development bootcamp funded by New York's Tech Talent Pipeline. I will always be a student at heart and enjoy learning new things.

In my free time, I can be found baking sweets for my loved ones and exploring new cultures through traveling. I have recently also been self-teaching myself UI/UX design and playing with Adobe XD on the side.
